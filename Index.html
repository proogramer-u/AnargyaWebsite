Engineering the Future of Electric Mobility

Anargya is the Formula Student electric racing team from Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember. Since 2018 we have been designing, validating, and assembling competitive electric race cars that showcase Indonesian engineering on the global stage.

Combining research-led innovation with hands-on craftsmanship, each season we refine our aerodynamics, chassis, powertrain, and control systems to push performance while cultivating the next generation of mobility leaders.

  • 40+ Active members from electrical, mechanical, and business disciplines.
  • 10+ International awards and recognitions across design and business events.
  • 2018 Established as the first electric Formula Student team from ITS Indonesia.
